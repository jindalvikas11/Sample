Our Approach to Privacy

 

Our goal is to maintain your trust and confidence when handling personal information about you.

Security of Personal Information

The security of personal information about you is our priority. We protect this information by maintaining physical, electronic and procedural safeguards that meet applicable law. We train our employees in the proper handling of personal information. When we use other companies to provide services for us, we require them to protect the confidentiality of personal information they receive.

Unfortunately, however, while we strive to protect your personal information, not everything is under our control and no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. You must keep your password and other website registration information you hold secret. You should also control access to your SMS/email communications at all times.

 1.            What Information Do We Collect?

When you visit our site, we automatically collect the IP address of the device you use to connect to the Internet. In addition, we gather information such as what browser and which version of it you're using, the type of operating system you have and  your internet service provider. If you access this website via your mobile device, we may also collect information about your mobile provider and your mobile device. This information allows us to recognize when your device accesses our website and helps us provide an online experience that matches your device.

We may use cookies or other tracking technologies to do this. In some cases, we may not be able to provide the services unless the cookie or other technology is installed.

In addition to the information described above , we may also gather or process personal information that you provide to us such as your user name, password, address, global location, card number, security code, postcode, email or telephone PIN.  In the course of using this website, you may provide us with other personal information to the extent you wish to do so or that we may request from you to allow us to provide you with further information. For example, where offered in your country, you may choose to provide us with your mobile number or email address in order to receive alerts relating to your reserved balance. Registration on ClientZone website is required in order to use your card. Registration on the One Platform website is not obligatory and you can still use your card without such registration (although you may not be able to receive all the services offered by us).

 

Cookies

When you browse our site, Citi uses cookies to collect information and store your online preferences.

Cookies are electronic browser files containing small amounts of information which are set on your device when you visit a website. Cookies are useful because they allow a website to recognize a user’s device.  Some of these cookies will be encrypted.

Most of the cookies used on the website are essential in order to enable you to access the website securely. This website uses temporary “session” cookies which are only active on your device for the duration of your usage of our website. These cookies may allow, for example, your device’s web browser to remember which pages on our website you have visited.

If you decide to tick the 'Remember my preferences'  box when logging in to our website, cookies may be placed on your device which remain on your device after you have finished using our website. These cookies may remain on your device until such time as you decide to delete them via your browser settings in accordance with Section 4 of this Online Privacy and Cookies Policy.  The details stored on these cookies may include your user name, language or the region you are in.  A cookie will also be set to record whether you have acknowledged our notice regarding the use of cookies, so that the notice won’t be displayed to you again unnecessarily.

It’s important to note that most of the cookies we use do not capture any information that can personally identify you and they cannot track your browsing activity on any other websites. Most of the cookies we use on the website are essential in order to enable you to access the website securely.

You can find more information about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set and how to manage and delete them, by looking at Section 4 of this Online Privacy and Cookies Policy, or by visiting the all about cookies website.

Where can I use this card?
You can use your card to purchase goods and services at any merchant that accepts signature based MasterCard® or Visa® debit cards (depending on the brand presented on the front of your card).
Why is the word "Debit" on the front of the card?
In response to new Visa and MasterCard regulations, Issuers are required to label non-credit products with the term “Debit” after January 1, 2004. Although your card may have the word debit on it, please remember to push credit and sign for purchases to indicate you authorize your transaction.
Can I withdraw cash?
Your card is a stored value card. You can use it to purchase goods and services, but there is no cash access. You will not be able to use your card for cash equivalent transactions.
How do I pay for the card?
If your card is assessed an annual or monthly fee, it will automatically be deducted from your balance. For a complete list of fees associated with your card, please refer to the Disclosures and Fees page.
